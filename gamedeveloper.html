Sports & Game Developer

As a member of the Game Development Unit of the Bangladesh Cricket Board (BCB), I focus on promoting cricket at the grassroots level. My role involves creating programs that enhance player skills and foster teamwork, ensuring the sustainable growth of cricket in our communities. I am dedicated to building a vibrant cricketing environment for aspiring athletes.

As a dedicated member of the BCB Game Development Unit, I strive to nurture young talent and promote cricket at the grassroots level, ensuring a strong foundation for the future of the sport.

  • Developing Grassroots Cricket Programs
  • Organizing Training Camps and Workshops
  • Mentoring Young Players and Coaches
  • Collaborating with Schools and Communities
  • Coordinating Local Tournaments and Leagues
  • Promoting Teamwork and Sportsmanship
