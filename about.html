Who am I?

Passionate Professional Cricket Coach | Ex Professional Cricketer | Councilor at BCB | Head Coach at Amirun Srity Cricket Academy

Contact Me

I provide personalized coaching to players of all ages, focusing on skill development, game strategy, and fostering a competitive spirit. My goal is to empower athletes to reach their full potential both on and off the field.

As the owner of four cricket clubs, I am actively involved in managing operations, promoting talent, and creating opportunities for players to excel in a supportive environment. My club is committed to nurturing the next generation of cricket stars.

I founded a cricket academy dedicated to providing comprehensive training programs. We focus on developing young talent through structured coaching sessions, skill assessments, and competitive play to prepare them for future challenges.

I oversee various business initiatives related to cricket, ensuring sustainable growth and investment in the sport. My focus is on creating opportunities for players and fans alike through innovative business models.

I organize cricket events and tournaments that bring together players and communities. These events not only foster competition but also promote camaraderie and a love for the game.

As being the member of BCB Game Development unit, I am involved in creating engaging and educational cricket-related content, utilizing technology to enhance player training and fan engagement. This includes developing resources that help players sharpen their skills and knowledge of the game.

What do I do?

Empowering the Future of Cricket: A Comprehensive Approach

In my role as a cricket coach and mentor, I am dedicated to shaping not just skilled players, but well-rounded individuals who embody the values of teamwork, discipline, and resilience. My mission is to inspire future generations while promoting the growth and development of cricket across the globe.
