About the Team

Champions in the Making: Azim Cricket Club's Pursuit of Excellence

Azim Cricket Club proudly stands as a formidable force in the Dhaka Cricket League's 1st Division. Under the adept leadership of Coach Tanvir Ahmed, we have transformed from our humble beginnings into a powerhouse of cricketing talent. Our players’ dedication and hard work have been pivotal in achieving remarkable success, including our ascension from the 2nd Division to the 1st Division after clinching the league championship in the 2017-2018 season. With numerous players earning spots in the esteemed Under 19 national team, Azim Cricket Club is committed to excellence and sportsmanship, inspiring cricket enthusiasts and aspiring cricketers alike. We invite you to witness our journey as we continue to strive for greatness on the field.
