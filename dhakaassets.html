About the Team

Unleashing Greatness: Get to Know the Dhaka Assets Cricket Club

Welcome to the Dhaka Assets Cricket Club, where passion meets precision, and dedication knows no bounds. As one of the leading cricket clubs in Bangladesh, we are committed to excellence both on and off the field. Our team comprises talented individuals who embody the spirit of sportsmanship and camaraderie, united by a common goal: to achieve greatness together. Led by the astute ownership of Coach Tanvir Ahmed, we strive to push the boundaries of our potential and inspire cricket enthusiasts across the nation. Join us on our journey as we continue to make waves in the world of cricket.

History of the Team

Established in 2017, the Dhaka Assets Cricket Club embarked on a remarkable journey from humble beginnings to soaring heights. Under the visionary leadership of Coach Tanvir Ahmed, our team quickly rose through the ranks, propelled by unwavering dedication and relentless determination. The turning point came in the 2018-2019 tournament season when we clinched the prestigious Champions Trophy in the Dhaka 2nd Division Cricket League, earning promotion to the esteemed first division. Since then, we have been a formidable presence in the Dhaka First Division Cricket League, leaving an indelible mark on the cricketing landscape with our exceptional performances and unwavering spirit. Our journey is a testament to the power of perseverance and teamwork, and our legacy continues to inspire generations of cricket enthusiasts.
